POPULAR MENU

Guacamole tradicional

A classic Mexican dip made with ripe avocados, lime, cilantro, onions, and tomatoes. It's fresh, flavorful, and often served with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, fajitas, and grilled meats.

Burrito Banderas

A burrito topped with three sauces representing the colors of the Mexican flag: green , white , and red . These sauces reflect the vibrant flavors, making the dish both visually striking and flavorful.

New El Don

Features raja tamale, sweet plantains, a crunchy chicken taco, Mexican nachos with sour cream, and fresh pico de gallo. It’s a flavorful and hearty combination of savory, sweet, and fresh elements.

Nachos Fajita

A delicious fusion of crispy nachos topped with fajita-style meat, peppers, onions, and melted cheese, often served with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole. It's a popular dish for parties or as an appetizer.

Esquites

Made from boiled or roasted corn kernels served in a cup. It's mixed with toppings like mayonnaise, Cotija cheese, lime, chili powder, and cilantro, offering a savory, creamy, and spicy flavor.

Cóctel de Camarones

A popular Mexican dish made with shrimp served in a tangy cocktail sauce, typically featuring tomato, lime, and chili. It's a refreshing, savory, and slightly spicy appetizer or light meal.

Elote Asado

It consist of grilled corn on the cob. The corn is typically charred over an open flame, enhancing its natural sweetness and flavor, and then topped with a variety of delicious ingredients.

Tacos de Camarón

Mexican tacos filled with seasoned shrimp, grilled, sautéed, or fried, and served in soft tortillas. They are topped with fresh ingredients like cabbage, pico de gallo, avocado, and creamy sauces.
